Designation: Omnisight Mapping Unit
Manufacturer: Akari Labs

Summary: Demolitionist-compatible devices containing mapping data for the current area. Provide both a general outline for your automap and also information on certain key points for your targetting array, such as the location of access keys, contraptions you can interact with, and also the nearest exits.
