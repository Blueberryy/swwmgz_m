Designation: Forx JetBurst Impulsor
Manufacturer: Forx Aeronautics

Summary: A portable, efficient jetpack unit. Allows the user to perform "extra jumps" in mid air, can also be used for rapid dashing and dodging. It is recommended to wear protective clothing to prevent damage from extended use.

Addendum: Its effectiveness isn't lost when submerged. As a matter of fact, it may be enhanced.
