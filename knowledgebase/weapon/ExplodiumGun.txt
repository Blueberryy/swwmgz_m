Designation: Explodium Gun
Manufacturer: Munch Innovations

Summary: Semi-automatic handgun chambered in custom .226 ETB (Explodium Tip Bullet).

Primary Fire: Slow, accurate shooting.

Secondary Fire: Rapid, less accurate shooting.

Reloading: Due to the sensitivity to impact of Explodium, if there are still bullets left in the mag it is possible to use it as a grenade.

Addendum: Thanks to a deal between Akari Labs and Munch Innovations, you have been provided with a practically limitless supply of ammunition for this weapon.
