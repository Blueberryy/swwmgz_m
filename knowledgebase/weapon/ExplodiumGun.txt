Designation: Explodium Gun
Manufacturer: Munch Innovations

Summary: Semi-automatic handgun designed for "Explodium Tipped Bullets".

Primary Fire: Slow, accurate shooting.

Secondary Fire: Performs an "explosive reload", pulling the pin on the mag to arm it as a grenade and throw it at enemies.

Reloading: Normal reload, nothing will explode here.

Addendum: Thanks to a deal between Akari Labs and Munch Innovations, you have been provided with a practically limitless supply of ammunition for this weapon.
